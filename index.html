Why Sanmitra?

The name is Sanskrit — chosen deliberately. In a world full of content designed to manipulate, we don't need another algorithm. We need a friend. A true friend who tells us what's actually in what we're consuming — not what we want to hear.

A Sanmitra in Indian philosophy speaks truth even when uncomfortable, guarding your wellbeing above momentary pleasure. Every score, every flag, every verdict is Sanmitra acting as your honest companion — helping you consume content that nourishes your mind, not poisons it.

My Principles
01

Truth before comfort

A real friend tells you when something is wrong — even when you don't want to hear it. Sanmitra scores content honestly, not generously. If a video spreads misinformation, the label says so.

02

Your values, your score

There is no universal definition of "good content." Sanmitra is built around personal value alignment — you define the filter, Sanmitra does the analysis.

03

Show the evidence, not just the verdict

Every score comes with full reasoning — transcript analysis, fact-checks, and compliance flags are all visible, because understanding makes you smarter, not just safer.

04

Access is a right, not a privilege

Media literacy has been the domain of experts. Sanmitra is free, requires no login, and runs in a browser. Good information hygiene belongs to everyone.
